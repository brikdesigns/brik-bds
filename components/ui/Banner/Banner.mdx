# Banner

<ComponentLinks slug="banner" />

Full-width contextual banner. `tone="announcement"` (default) renders a brand-primary surface for marketing notices; `tone="warning" | "error" | "information"` renders a secondary surface with a leading status Badge and `role="alert"`.
