# ProductSummaryCard

<ComponentLinks slug="product-summary-card" />

Compact summary of a selected product or service — a leading `ServiceTag` (coloured per `serviceLine`), a `label` caption, the offering `value`, and an optional `price • frequency` line. Used to echo back the offering a visitor selected.

## Default

<Canvas of={Stories.Default} />

## Variants

The card renders whatever offering it's given; `serviceLine` recolours the tag and `frequency` switches the price line between one-time and recurring.
